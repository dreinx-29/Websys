About Us

Welcome to 5A's General Merchandise, your one-stop destination for delightful finds and everyday essentials! Conveniently located at Arayat Public Market along General Alejandrino Street, Arayat, Pampanga, we proudly offer a wide variety of products to meet your daily needs and indulgent cravings. From sweet treats and local delicacies to toys, candies, and more, our shop is filled with little treasures that bring joy to every visit.

Whether you're picking up a snack, looking for something fun, or exploring what’s new, 5A’s is where quality meets variety-served with a warm smile and genuine care.
